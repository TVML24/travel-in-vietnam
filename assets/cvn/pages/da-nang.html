Da Nang

The tourism capital of central Vietnam

What to do

  • Visit Asia Park - Although the park in Ba Na hills may be slightly more famous, this one is certainly still worth visiting with a great variety of rides, attractions and photo opportunities.
  • Stroll along My Khe Beach - Although it's not the best beach in Vietnam for swimming it's definitely one that has great views and really allows you to imagine how Da Nang emerged from a small fishing port to a tourist behemoth.
  • See the towering Marble Mountains - Featuring a collection of galleries to buy statues made of the famous marble, walking trails along each that showcase their association with buddhism and a collection of pagodas and monestaries.

What to eat and drink

  • Fine dining - Tamarind Crab (Cua Rang Me); as with most coastal cities in Vietnam, the seafood is definitely the highlight. This dish in particular will set your mouth watering as the meaty crab flavour meets the sweet and sour influence of the tamarind paste.
  • From the street - Quang-Style Noodles (Mi Quang) is the speciality noodle dish of the city and is not be missed. Featuring noodles, banana blossom, quail eggs and peanut-style broth; These noodles are not to be missed.
  • For the adventurous - Avocado Icecream (Kem Bo) is a different take on a classic dessert and definitely one worth trying. The avocado is layered with coconut icecream before blending. One more delightful way to cool down.
